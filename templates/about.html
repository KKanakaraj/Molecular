About

Our project has the aim to highlight how hormones could influence people day life, since they have an important impact on social behavior. We pointed out the main features related to oxytocin, dopamine, estrogen, testosterone and vasopressin. Them all are highly involved in social disorders such as aggressivity, anxiety, depression, schizophrenia. For each of these hormones we carried out a brief summary of their biological mechanism followed by a detailed explanation of their role in these type of disorders.
